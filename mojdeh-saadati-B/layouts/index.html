Welcome to my website!

My name is Mojdeh Saadati, and I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Iowa State University of Science and Technology- College of Liberal Arts and Sciences. My research interest lies in the study of the structure of machine learning software along with its application in various domains, specifically social good and public health. In my Ph.D., I am focusing on improving machine learning software to mitigate social biases in their decision-making. Also, I devoted my master to study causal inference and missingness mechanism, two fundamental problems in data science. As a result of this research, I devised an algorithm to measure the causal effect of two sets of variables under data with an arbitrary missing mechanism. During my research experience, I have collaborated on several public health projects published in top medical journals.
